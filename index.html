Rice Breeds:


Arborio Rice

Arborio rice is a medium-grain Italian rice known for its starchy and creamy texture. It's commonly used in risotto dishes.

Basmati Rice

Basmati rice is a fragrant, long-grain rice with a delicate aroma. It's popular in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisines.


Ipsala Rice

Ipsala rice is a short-grain rice variety grown in Turkey. It's known for its versatility in various dishes.


Jasmine Rice

Jasmine rice is a fragrant, long-grain rice commonly used in Southeast Asian cuisine. It has a subtly sweet aroma.


Karacadag Rice

Karacadag rice is a unique variety from Turkey with its own distinct flavor and characteristics.



About Our Project

Our project aims to automate the classification of rice varieties using artificial intelligence and computer vision. By uploading an image of a rice grain, our system can predict its variety among the five breeds listed above. This technology not only simplifies the process but also empowers farmers, researchers, and consumers with valuable insights into rice diversity.
